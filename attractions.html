Tourist Attraction & Delicacies

Discover the captivating tourist attractions and mouthwatering delicacies that make every visit unforgettable.

Cambugahay Falls

One of Siquijor's most famous waterfalls with crystal-clear blue waters and rope swings.

Salagdoong Beach

A beautiful white-sand beach known for cliff diving and turquoise waters.

Century-Old Balete Tree

A mystical tree with a natural spring at its base, famous for its eerie yet serene vibe.

Lazi Church & Convent

A historical landmark showcasing Spanish colonial architecture and religious heritage.

Paliton Beach

A serene stretch of white sand and crystal-clear waters, perfect for sunset views and relaxing escapes.

Butterfly Sanctuary

A tranquil haven showcasing vibrant butterfly species amidst lush greenery.

Torta

A delectable local delicacy, this sweet and buttery pastry is a must-try treat in Siquijor.

Tinalay or Pan Bisaya

A traditional bread made with love, embodying the rich culinary heritage of Siquijor.

Tubod Marine Sanctuary

A vibrant underwater haven teeming with colorful coral reefs and diverse marine life, perfect for snorkeling and diving.
